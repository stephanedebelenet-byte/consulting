# Case Study: OCP Group SAEDM — 10-Year Transformation Journey

**Client:** OCP Group / SAEDM (Filiale)  
**Scope:** Building modern Procurement function from ground up  
**Duration:** 10 years ongoing  

## Initial Baseline (2016)
- Ad-hoc procurement processes  
- Limited digitalization  
- Governance scattered  
- Fournisseurs: Hundreds, unmanaged  

## Transformation Phases
### Phase 1-2: Foundation (Years 1-2)
- Governance structure design  
- Process standardization  
- Standards alignment (Groupe requirements)  

### Phase 3-4: Digitalization (Years 3-5)
- Architecture digitale (flux dématérialisés)  
- Supplier platform implementation  
- Real-time visibility  

### Phase 5+: Continuous Improvement (Years 5-10)
- Agilité supply chain  
- Écosystème complexe management  
- Strategic sourcing evolution  

## Key Achievements (2016-2026)
- Conformité: 98%  
- Coûts: Optimized & controlled  
- Résilience: Écosystèmes complexes managed  
- Équipe: Moderne, digitally-enabled
