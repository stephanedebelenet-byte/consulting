# Case Study: Diana Holding — Multi-Subsidiary S&OP Integration

**Client:** Diana Holding (diversified: Boissons + Agro)  
**Scope:** S&OP implementation across 4 subsidiaries  
**Challenge:** Different business models, supply chains not integrated  

## Baseline
- Filiales planned independently  
- Stock: 35% safety buffer  
- Ruptures: 8%  
- Cash cycle: 52 days  

## Intervention (8 weeks)
- Unified S&OP process design  
- Demand + Supply alignment training  
- Group-level steering committee  

## Results
- Stock: 35% → 18% (-48%)
- Ruptures: 8% → 2%  
- Cash cycle: 52 → 40 days (-12 days)
- Cash freed: 2.1 MMAD
