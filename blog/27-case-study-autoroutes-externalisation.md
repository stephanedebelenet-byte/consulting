# Case Study: Autoroutes du Maroc — Critical Externalisation Pilotage

**Client:** Autoroutes du Maroc  
**Scope:** Péage & Assistance externalisation (1.2 MMAD annual flow)  
**Duration:** 11 months  
**Objective:** SLA/KPI contractual framework  

## Baseline
- Contrats "classiques" (pas de SLA clair)  
- Coûts: +8% drift vs forecast  
- Risques: Non-gérés  

## Intervention
- Contractual framework redesign (SLA/KPI centered)  
- Vendor management process  
- Risk mitigation strategy  
- Governance structure  

## Results
- Coûts: Stables (vs +8% expected)  
- SLA: 99.2% compliance  
- Risques: Mitigated & monitored
